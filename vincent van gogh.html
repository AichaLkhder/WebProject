Starry Night

By Vincent van Gogh

Starry Night

This masterpiece was painted in 1889. It depicts a dreamy interpretation of the artist's asylum room's sweeping view of Saint-Rémy-de-Provence at night, enhanced by swirling stars and a glowing crescent moon.

the bedroom

By Vincent van Gogh

the bedroom

A painting from 1888 of Van Gogh’s simple bedroom in Arles, characterized by its vivid colors and geometric shapes, reflecting his quest for peace and stability.

Café Terrace at Night

By Vincent van Gogh

Café Terrace at Night

A lively night scene from 1888 set in Arles, featuring a café bathed in warm yellow light against a starry sky, showing Van Gogh's fascination with nighttime.

Sunflowers

By Vincent van Gogh

Sunflowers

A series of vibrant still-lifes from 1888 of sunflowers in vases, symbolizing gratitude and the cycle of life, painted with bold and expressive brushstrokes.

Wheatfield with Crows

By Vincent van Gogh

Wheatfield with Crows

One of Van Gogh's last works from 1890, depicting a turbulent sky over a wheat field, with dark crows symbolizing isolation and inner turmoil.
